Interactive Todo List

How to use:

1. Click the ✓ icon at the end of each todo item

2. The content slides left, revealing "CONFIRM" button from the right

3. Click "CONFIRM" to complete the todo with fireworks!


Configurable Items:

Items can be loaded from a server with custom:

  • Background colors
  • Text colors
  • Custom styling

Open browser console to see examples of server-loaded items.
